STAY CONNECTED

Stay connected with DAE and your friends and former fellow students!

 

Join the private DAE Veterans page on Facebook. The place to be to chit-chat, post interesting bits of news and job offers,… And most of all, where we keep contact, set up and communicate events, parties and alumni get-togethers. 

Join us on Linked In through DAE Alumni or let Howest know about your whereabouts through this link.

 

We love to keep track of you, so if you change jobs, have a career switch or you are taking a five year holiday to be involved in a crazy project, it would be great if you could drop us a line through one of the above channels.

 

SHOW US YOUR WORK

We are always keen to know on what your are working on, so let us know if a game, a commercial, a movie or any other project is released and carries your name, so we can feature it on our website. It can inspire new DAE students to push their limits. You can email sandra.veresa@howest.be for any news topics.
